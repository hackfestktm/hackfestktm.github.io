About the hackathon

A hackathon is a full-day event where students imagine, design, develop, and present a product from scratch (and win cool prizes). The goal is for teams to take an idea from inception to completion and present their accomplishments. Hack Fest Kathmandu is a platform where student community will come together to build innovative solutions that solve real problems.


  • Promote creativity & team working
  • Provide students with mentorship and guidance from professionals
  • Build innovative solutions

FAQs

Who can participate?

We are focusing this hackathon for the high school students related to IT. So, if you are a bachelor level student or just completed your bachelor degree, you can join the hackathon. A team of great mentors will be there to advise and guide you each step of the way to complete your project.

How big can be a team ?

You need at least 2 meambers to form a team. You can form teams of up to 5 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

What if I don't have a team or an idea ?

Not to worry, most people don’t! We’ll have team formation and ideathon event geared towards helping you find people to work with. You can join our Facebook Group to find potential teammates.

What should I bring ?

You should probably bring laptop and charger.

What is the cost of the event ?

The cost of registration is Rs. 300 per participant.

Do I need programming experience ?

Hack Fest Kathmandu is a great way to begin your programming experience! A basic understanding is recommended, but we will have mentors to help you make your project idea a reality.

How can I get involved if I am not participating ?

If you're looking to be a volunteer or mentor (i.e. someone with technical skills who'd be willing to help out participants during the weekend), you should reach out to us in our Facebook Group



Introduction

A hackathon is an event that gathers programmers and tech enthusiasts with different skill sets to “hack”, or work collaboratively, on a software project.


During the event, each team will develop a solution focussing on one of the challenges provided. Each team will be assigned mentors for assistance in the project they are building. Hackers can use web or mobile platform using programming language of their choice to work on their project.
